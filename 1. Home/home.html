Context & Intructions

This website is part of our research project that attempts to teach about the pollution and conservation efforts in Nashville while also teaching a little about the city's history. At this point, you should already have taken a little survey about that and figured out how to get here. Now, follow the the next instructions, but make sure to read all the instructions before doing any of them:

  1. Go to the Map of Nashville below.
  2. Double click on the blue marker closest to the top left corner.
  3. Read the paragraphs about the location the marker corresponded to.
  4. Use a the Next button to move to the next page. Alternatively, you can go back to the home page (click on the three horizontal lines on the top right and then click Home) and double click in another blue marker.
  5. Repeat step 3 with the remaining 3 pages.
  6. If you would like to get extra information on pollution effects and what you can do to help, visit the sources and the page for action steps by clicking in the three horizontal lines on the top right and clicking Sources or Action Steps (Optional).
  7. When you are done reading all the pages, please take the second part of the survey.
