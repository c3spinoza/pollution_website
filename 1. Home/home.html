Context & Intructions

This website part of our research project that attemps to teach about the pollution and conservation efforts in Nashville while teaching a little about the city's history. At this point, you should have already take a little survey that and already figured out how to get here. Now, follow the the next instructions, but make sure to read all the instruction before doign any of them:

  1. Go to the Map of Nashville below.
  2. Double click on the blue marker closest to the top left corner.
  3. Read the paragraphs about the location that marker corresponded to and answer the question at the end.
  4. Go back to the home age by clicking on the hamburger menu on the top right of the website and then clicking Home.
  5. Repeat steps 2 and 3 but with the remaining 3 markers (attempt to move the top left to the bottom right).
  6. Visit the sources and the page for action steps by clicking in the hamburger menu and clicking Sources or Action Steps (Optional).
  7. Done reading all the pages and answering the questions, take the second part of the survey.
