Context & Intructions

This website is part of our research project that attempts to teach about the pollution and conservation efforts in Nashville while also teaching a little about the city's history. At this point, you should already have taken a little survey about that and figured out how to get here. Now, follow the the next instructions, but make sure to read all the instructions before doing any of them:

  1. Go to the Map of Nashville below.
  2. Double click on the blue marker closest to the top left corner.
  3. Read the paragraphs about the location that that marker corresponded to and answer the question(s) at the end.
  4. Go back to the home page by clicking on the hamburger menu on the top right of the website and then clicking Home.
  5. Repeat steps 2 and 3 but with the remaining 3 markers (attempt to move the top left to the bottom right).
  6. Visit the sources and the page for action steps by clicking in the hamburger menu and clicking Sources or Action Steps (Optional).
  7. When you are done reading all the pages and answering the questions, please take the second part of the survey.
